About

Welcome to Project For Everyone! Our goal is to provide a space where you can develop your skills, learn new skills or kickstart your dream career through various projects and job simulation scenarios, our platform is designed to help you feel more confident in your skills as well as being more confident when applying for roles you may want to specialise on.

Assistance

If you need help with anything, feel free to reach out to our support team. We are here to assist you with any questions or issues you may have. Contact us at projectforeveryoneassistance@gmail.com.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I upload a project?

To upload a project, navigate to the Projects page, fill out the form, and submit your project.

How can I find collaborators?

Browse through the projects listed on the main page and reach out to project owners via the contact details provided.

What file formats are supported?

You can upload files in any format, but common formats like .zip, .rar, .pdf, .docx, and .png are recommended for easier access.
